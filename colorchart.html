Color Samples Warm, Cool, Neutral Details Page

White is color at its most complete and pure, the color of perfection. The color meaning of white is purity, innocence, wholeness and completion. White contains an equal balance of all the colors of the spectrum, representing both the positive and negative aspects of all colors. Its basic feature is equality, implying fairness and impartiality, neutrality and independence.
