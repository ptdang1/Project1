About The Color Gray.

 

 

 

Gray is the color of intellect, knowledge, and wisdom. It is perceived as long-lasting, classic, and often as sleek or refined. It is a color that is dignified, conservative, and carries authority.1

Gray is a beautiful color. It is a cool, conservative color that seldom evokes strong emotion although it can be seen as a cloudy or moody color. All shades of gray can be good, neutral background colors.2

Gray has steadily grown as one of the top neutrals in decorating. Unlike beige neutrals, gray offers a unique ability to pair with the muted candy tones of pink, yellow, and blue. Color trends have been featuring these soft colors for a few years, with gray as the perfect complement. I think of using gray with muted colors in the same way that we match the volume of our voice to the people around us in a room.3
